Hello!

I'm a library and information science professional who's worked in academic libraries and archives since 2018. I specialize in digital collections and repositories.

My mission is to make UNLV outputs available for users across the globe, in perpetuity, with as few barriers to access and reuse as possible.

Areas of interest include:

- institutional repositories
- open access
